Call for Papers

The Programme will cover three days, Monday March 13 through Wednesday March 15, 2017. It is expected at this stage that each Day will consist of four sessions, between 9 am and 6 pm, with four 30-min presentations in each session. Depending on the number of registered participants, duration of presentations may be reduced and poster session introduced. Discussion session, to summarise the symposium outcomes and to plan further actions, will also be organised.

We expect a submission of an abstract only, to designate its Main Theme. Please note that the Symposium will not have a Proceedings Volume.
